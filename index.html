Group and Channel Submission

Users may submit groups and channels from supported platforms including:

  • WhatsApp Groups
  • WhatsApp Channels
  • Telegram Groups
  • Telegram Channels
  • Facebook Groups
  • Messenger Groups
  • Messenger Channels

When submitting a group or channel:

  1. The user must provide a valid invite or public link.
  2. The system automatically detects the platform type.
  3. Unsupported or invalid links may be rejected automatically.
  4. After a supported link is accepted, the user may add group/channel name, description, image from gallery, and category.
  5. Location permission may be requested to help display groups relevant to nearby users.

Location-Based Search

Smart Group Join allows users to discover groups and channels based on city, country, or nearby location.

When a user searches for a specific city or area, only groups and channels associated with that location may be displayed.

If no groups or channels are available for the searched location, no results may be shown.
